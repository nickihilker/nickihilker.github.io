FroggerProject

Frogger Python Project

Description

My team made a game based on the game Frogger. You can move up, down, left, and right, and you can't go outside of the margins of the screen. There is a player, that is a green dot, and three enemies. The enemies move back and forth at different speeds. When the player touches one of the enemies, it is sent back to where it began, and a live is taken away. It started with 5 lives. When the lives are down to 0, the enemies stop moving.


The github repository can be found here



This is the start of the game. You can move the objects left, right, up, and down, using the W, A, S, and D keys. Left=A, Right=D, Up=W, and Down=S. The logs (rectangles) move at different speeds and different ways. You win by getting to the top of the screen.



This is after you lose the game. You lose by touching the logs five times. Once you hit them five times, everything stops, and the game is over.



This was our hardest piece of coding. The player needs to be sent back to start when it hits an enemy, which is what this coding shows. My team made a small black square with the same coordinates that the player started at.
